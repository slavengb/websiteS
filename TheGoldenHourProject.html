Dog waste, full of cracks, remnants of digested food, micro details and junk of all kinds that up close and enlarged to a bigger scale appears to talk about some distant past.

Looking into the future and, at the same time, exploring the past through clumsy shapes, crude subject and almost ritualistically set dog fouling,
the work tries to wander through the time of humanity's so-called progress and explores the human behaviour.
