This work is an exploration and response to the inextricable link between life and death and the struggle to make sense of the relationship of the two.
In response to our fragile and short life we attempt to somehow reach and grasp the eternal and everlasting, one cannot help but to see this tragi-comic event on a daily basis.
At the same time funny and sad, beautiful and ugly, abstract and actual.
A world between opposites calling for contemplation.
